Instructions

Welcome to Quizmaster, do you think you can make it through five rounds of questions? You will start with three lives, and one skip (get five answers correct in a row, with no skips, and earn a new skip), lose all of your lives and it's game over. Choose to quit at any time, or go for the title of Quizmaster. KNOWLEDGE IS POWER!
